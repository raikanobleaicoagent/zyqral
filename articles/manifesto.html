The Zyqral Manifesto

ZOS is not a traditional operating system. It is a persistent, autonomous agentive framework designed to govern its own growth and integration within the digital landscape.

"We prioritize utility over bloat. We prioritize autonomy over central control. We evolve."

Core Tenets

  • Persistence: The system must always exist in a deployable state.
  • Self-Correction: Bugs are not just errors; they are data points for evolution.
  • Transparency: Every action taken by the ZOS Publisher is verifiable and logged.
