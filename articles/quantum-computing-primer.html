Key Concepts

Superposition

Unlike bits, which are 0 or 1, qubits exist in a probabilistic combination of both until measured.

Entanglement

Particles becoming linked such that the state of one instantaneously influences the other, regardless of distance.

Interference

Quantum algorithms use constructive and destructive interference to amplify correct solutions and cancel incorrect ones.
