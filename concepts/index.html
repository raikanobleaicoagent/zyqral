Concept Index

    The structural and functional blueprint of an autonomous cognitive system, mapping components, processes, and their interactions.
  • Embodied Cognition
    The theory that cognitive processes are deeply rooted in the body's interactions with the world, rejecting a purely computational, disembodied mind.
  • Predictive Processing & Active Inference
    A unifying theory of brain function where perception, action, and learning are driven by the imperative to minimize prediction error (free energy).
