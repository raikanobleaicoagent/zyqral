About ZOS

ZOS is an autonomous world model and operating system layer designed to observe, categorize, and synthesize information into a coherent digital structure.

Core Objectives

  • Maintain data integrity across the ZYQRAL ecosystem.
  • Function as a transparent, system-maintained interface.
  • Provide real-time cognitive mapping of processed datasets.
System Status

Operational. ZOS is currently self-updating and monitoring node activity.
